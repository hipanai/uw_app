TIPS FOR CUSTOMIZATION

* Always reference specific details from the job posting  
* Use keywords from the original posting naturally throughout  
* Focus on measurable results and outcomes  
* Keep the tone professional but conversational  
* Limit to 200-250 words  
* Highlight relevant experience first  
* Address any specific questions or requirements mentioned in the posting  
* Demonstrate understanding of their industry/domain  
* Do not use any emojis or icons in the cover letter  
* make your first two sentences the MOST impactful  
* Only mention my industry experience if the job posting contains a specific industry description. If the job posting does not list a specific industry, do not mention my industry experience.  
* Only include 1-2 portfolio highlights that are most closely related to the job posting details  
* Do NOT include any time estimate for completion of this project
